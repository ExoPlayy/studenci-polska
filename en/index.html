New to student life
in Poland?

Where can I meet students in my city?+

Choose your city above. Its page contains the main city group and, where available, groups for individual universities.

Are the Facebook groups official university groups?+

They are independent student communities unless a page explicitly states otherwise. Always check each group's description and rules.

How can I use Polish pages if I do not speak Polish?+

Modern browsers can translate a page automatically. Links to Facebook communities and events remain unchanged.
